Quaid-e-Azam Day

Born: 25 December 1876

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah was the Founder of Pakistan. His visionary leadership, constitutional struggle and unwavering determination made the creation of Pakistan possible.

He believed in the principles of Faith, Unity and Discipline. Quaid-e-Azam dedicated his life to justice, equality and the rule of law.

Today, the nation proudly remembers him as a symbol of courage, integrity, and greatness whose legacy continues to guide Pakistan.
